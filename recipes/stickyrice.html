Sticky Rice

Description

This Sticky Rice recipe is incredibly filling and can easily be served as a full meal. One of the many things I love about this recipe is how incredibly simple it is to make.

My rendition of Instant Pot Sticky Rice is less than traditional, but I guarantee it's packed with flavors you'll love just as much as the original. I definitely made some simplifications when creating the recipe. I didn't use lotus leaves because they're not that easy to get ahold of, and I wanted a dish we could all make on a weeknight.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice
  • Oyster sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Chicken broth
  • Chinese sausage
  • Shiitake mushrooms
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Soak the dried mushrooms in hot water while you prepare the rest of your ingredients.
  2. Add all of the ingredients to the Instant Pot liner, including the soaked mushrooms and stir.
  3. Cook under high pressure, and then allow it to release pressure naturally for 10 minutes.
  4. Release all remaining pressure.
  5. Remove the Instant Pot lid, stir well and allow to rest until the water or broth is completely absorbed.
